WorldofWordSearch

WorldofWordSearch is a game made is using dart
There is currently 3 diffrent levels to this game, on the first level the bord is randomly generating words between 3-5 characters long on a bord that 3x3-5x5 characters long, the second level the words are generating words between 6-8 characters long on a bord that is 6x6-8x8 characters long, on the hard level its generating word on a bord that is 9x9-13x13 characters long.
The current game has a bad word filter that filters out bad word that are accidentally created upon generating the bord.
